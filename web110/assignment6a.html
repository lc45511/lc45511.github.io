While completing the multimedia activities in this lesson, I learned how to embed images, audio, and video into web pages and how these elements can enhance a website’s effectiveness and visual appeal. I gained a better understanding of the differences between raster images, vector images, and AI-generated images, as well as how multimedia can improve user engagement and communication. I also learned how multimedia content can be integrated into web pages to create a more interactive user experience. In accounting, these skills can be used to create professional websites that present financial information, reports, and services in a clear and engaging manner. In marketing, multimedia content can help attract customers, strengthen brand awareness, and increase the effectiveness of digital advertising campaigns. More generally, understanding how to use multimedia on websites can improve communication, increase audience engagement, and create more effective online experiences in a wide variety of personal and professional settings.
