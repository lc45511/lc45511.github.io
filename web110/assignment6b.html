Activity: Web Accessibility

The WAVE report indicated that the paragraph text had insufficient color contrast on both the external and internal style pages. It also identified that my image on Assignment 6a was missing alternative (alt) text. For each error, WAVE provides an explanation of what the issue means, why it matters, and how to fix it. It also includes links to general accessibility guidelines to help make websites more accessible.

For the external style, I made the heading and paragraph text colors darker by changing the heading color from DarkOrchid to Violet and the paragraph color from MediumOrchid to DarkMagenta. For the internal style on Assignment 5b, I made the heading and paragraph text colors darker by changing the heading color from DarkOliveGreen to Black and the paragraph color from Olive to DarkSlateGray. I also added alt text to my image on Assignment 6a: Hand holding a magnifying glass over a balance sheet diagram, highlighting the "cash" section within the "assets" category. The W3C HTML Validator identified that the quotation marks in my alt text on Assignment 6a were interfering with the HTML. It also indicated that my video file, "Inserting a Video File," needed to be renamed to remove the spaces from the filename. In addition, the embedded YouTube video contained the obsolete frameborder="0" attribute, so I removed it. When I used the W3C CSS Validator, no errors were found.

After making these changes, every assignment page, including my index page, received a perfect AIM accessibility score of 10/10. The W3C HTML Validator reported no errors or warnings, and the W3C CSS Validator also reported no errors. I can apply this knowledge in accounting by creating accessible websites that allow clients to easily view financial information and reports. In marketing, accessible websites can reach a broader audience and improve the user experience. More generally, understanding web accessibility helps businesses create inclusive, professional websites that are easier for everyone to use, improving communication and customer satisfaction.
