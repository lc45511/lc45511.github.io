Activity: Project Management

For my final project, I plan to create a multimedia presentation about Internet Safety and Cybersecurity. This topic combines many of the concepts I learned throughout the course and focuses on practical ways individuals and businesses can protect themselves while using the internet. The presentation will explain the importance of creating strong, unique passwords, using password managers to securely store login credentials, and enabling multi-factor authentication (MFA) to add an extra layer of account protection. I will also discuss common cyber threats, including phishing emails, malware, worms, viruses, and spyware, while explaining how users can recognize warning signs and avoid becoming victims.

While completing the activities in Assignment 4b and Activity 1 of Assignment 3, I developed a much stronger understanding of internet safety and cybersecurity and how they help protect people, businesses, and information in today's digital world. One of the most valuable lessons I learned was how to identify phishing emails by looking for suspicious sender addresses, unexpected attachments, urgent requests for personal information, poor grammar, and misleading links. These concepts can be directly applied in accounting by helping safeguard financial records, payroll systems, tax information, and confidential client data from cyber threats. In marketing, cybersecurity helps protect customer information, company websites, digital advertising accounts, and social media platforms while maintaining consumer trust and protecting an organization's reputation. More generally, these skills will help me make safer decisions online, reduce the risk of cyberattacks, and prepare me for future careers in which protecting digital information and understanding cybersecurity best practices are essential responsibilities.
