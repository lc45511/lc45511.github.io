Activity 1: Search Engines Tutorials

The first activity was completing four different tutorials: LearnFree: Search Better, TutorialsPoint: Search Engine, LearnFree: ChatGPT for Beginners, and LearnFree: A Beginner's Guide to SEO Basics. These tutorials allowed me to explore tips for using search engines more effectively, gain a stronger understanding of what search engines are and how they work, learn practical ways to use ChatGPT, and recognize the importance of SEO.

LearnFree: Search Better focused primarily on Google, the most widely used search engine. It provided several strategies to keep in mind while searching and explained how to better understand and evaluate search results. TutorialsPoint: Search Engine offered a broader perspective on search engines as a whole. This learning activity explained the purpose of search engines and how they help users locate information on the internet. The tutorial covered the major components of a search engine, including web crawlers, indexes, search algorithms, and user interfaces. It also described the three stages of how search engines work: web crawling, web indexing, and searching and ranking. In addition, the tutorial introduced the concept of Search Engine Optimization (SEO), which was explored further in LearnFree: A Beginner's Guide to SEO Basics. It also provided brief descriptions of several popular search engines. LearnFree: ChatGPT for Beginners was a series of videos covering different ways to use ChatGPT. The videos began with a general overview of its use cases and advantages before focusing on specifics, such as creating an account, writing prompts, attaching files, and getting started with the platform. The final tutorial, LearnFree: A Beginner's Guide to SEO Basics, was shorter but emphasized important concepts such as focusing on user intent instead of stuffing content with keywords, the importance of content quality and structure, and maintaining consistency.

There are various ways these concepts can be applied. Personally, I use advanced search features frequently when working on research papers because they allow me to narrow results to the correct time period and filter topics enough to make them useful for my purposes, which is a skill that can be valuable in any career. Translation features can also be useful in business settings when quick clarification is needed while communicating with someone who speaks another language. I previously took an AI marketing class here at Harper College that helped me connect the different ways AI tools like ChatGPT can refine ideas, generate outlines, and improve efficiency. SEO, in particular, is crucial in marketing because it helps businesses improve visibility, increase engagement, and connect with their intended audience. Understanding how these tools work will help me conduct better research, communicate more effectively, and use digital technologies more strategically in my future career.
